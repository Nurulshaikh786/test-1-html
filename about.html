About Porsche 911

Overview

  • Manufacturer: Porsche AG
  • Production: Since September 1964
  • Assembly: Stuttgart, Germany
  • Body and Chassis: Sports car / Grand tourer (S)
  • Body Style: 2-door, 2+2 or 2-seat coup√©, convertible, targa top, speedster
  • Layout: Rear-engine, rear-wheel drive / all-wheel drive

Performance

  • Engine: Rear-mounted flat-six engine
  • Power Output: Ranges from 380 bhp to 641 bhp depending on the model
  • Acceleration: 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph) in 2.7 to 4.2 seconds
  • Top Speed: 293 km/h to 330 km/h (183 mph to 205 mph)

Variants

  • 911 Carrera: Base model with a 3.0-litre turbocharged flat-six engine
  • 911 GTS: More powerful version with a 3.6-litre engine
  • 911 Turbo: High-performance model with a 3.8-litre turbocharged engine
  • 911 GT3: Track-focused variant with a 4.0-litre naturally aspirated engine
  • 911 GT3 RS: Even more track-focused with enhanced performance features

Features

  • Rear-Axle Steering: Enhances driving stability and maneuverability
  • T-Hybrid Technology: Combines a traditional engine with an electric exhaust turbocharger for better performance
  • Safety: Equipped with multiple airbags and advanced safety features

Price

  • Price Range: Approximately Rs. 1.99 Crore to Rs. 4.26 Crore (depending on the variant)
